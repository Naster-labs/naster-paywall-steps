# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as project contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of experience level, background, education, personal identity, or beliefs.

We are building **learning-first, inclusive open-source software**. Mistakes,
questions, learning, and curiosity are encouraged.

## Our Standards

### We commit to:

- Being respectful and welcoming
- Giving constructive and actionable feedback
- Supporting beginners and learners
- Encouraging new ideas and perspectives
- Taking responsibility for our mistakes

### We do not tolerate:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or belittling behavior
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive conduct
- Sharing private information without consent

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers may:

- Clarify and enforce community standards
- Remove comments, code, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Ban temporary or permanent contributors for unacceptable behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions
- Community communications (Discord, email, etc.)
- Project events and learning environments
